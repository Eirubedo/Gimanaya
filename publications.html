Feelings behind words: A systematic review on how effective is NLP-based assessment for mental health diagnosis in human studies

Standardized Protocol Clinical Nursing

Psychiatric Nurse-Led Assertiveness Therapy: A Standardized Protocol for the Reduction of Violent Behavior in Schizophrenia

A five-session nurse-led assertiveness training combined with standard care significantly enhanced hospitalized schizophrenia patients' ability to recognize and control violent behavior, resulting in robust reductions in their overall behavioral symptoms.

Development & Evaluation VR Therapy Open Access PDF

Improving or worsening?: the development and evaluation of a VR-based psychotherapy to bullying victims and perpetrators in school adolescents

A four-session VR-based emotional regulation intervention reduced self-perceived bullying behaviors among adolescents, particularly males. However, it also increased self-reported emotional difficulties, likely by enhancing self-awareness of bullying experiences. Overall, students rated the VR module positively, suggesting that VR may be an effective anti-bullying tool when combined with appropriate counseling and support services.

Published in JNERS (Universitas Airlangga) • School Mental Health & VR Intervention Access Full Article open_in_new
