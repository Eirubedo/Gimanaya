PeriXa Batin: VR-Based Therapy for Youth Mental Health

Our team at FIK UI designed PeriXa Batin, a safe, 5-minute virtual reality therapy application. It utilizes personality validations (Myers-Briggs, Enneagram, Jungian Archetypes) to tailor sessions that trigger positive cognitive responses for anxiety reduction.
